About me

Hi! Formally, my name is Joshua Tan, but I tend to just go by Josh. I am currently in my second year at Bocconi University, pursuing a bachelor's degree in mathematical and computing sciences for artificial intelligence. It's a mouthful, I know.

Before moving to Milan, I spent my whole life in Vancouver, Canada. It was in my hometown that I fostered my desire to learn more about the world. Whether it be programming a (rudimentary) role-playing video game from scratch in elementary school, teaching myself how to play guitar, or self-studying languages like French and Italian, I've never been one to shy away from the daunting challenge of learning a new discipline independently.
